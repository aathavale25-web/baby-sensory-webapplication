# Parent's Guide to Baby Sensory World

A complete guide for parents on how to use Baby Sensory World with your little one.

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Open the app** in your web browser
2. **Create a profile** for your baby:
   - Enter their name
   - Select their age (4-12 months)
3. **Start playing!** The app automatically adapts to their age

## How to Play Together

### Interactive Tips

**Be Engaged**
- Sit with your baby
- Point at objects and colors
- Say the colors: "Look, red!" "See the blue circle?"
- Clap when they touch something
- Mirror their excitement

**Encourage Exploration**
- Guide their hand to the screen
- Celebrate each touch: "You did it!"
- Let them lead the pace
- Follow their interest

**Narrate the Experience**
- "The fish is swimming!"
- "Let's touch the star"
- "Wow, it's getting bigger!"
- Count objects together

### Age-Specific Guidance

#### 4-6 Months: Discovery Phase

**What to Expect**
- May swipe randomly at first
- Learning to track objects
- Building hand-eye coordination
- Short attention spans (5-10 minutes)

**How to Help**
- Hold their attention with your voice
- Guide their hand gently
- Celebrate every attempt
- Keep sessions short

**Activities**
- Track objects together with your finger
- Make sounds when objects appear
- Gentle hand-over-hand touches
- Name colors simply

#### 7-9 Months: Engagement Phase

**What to Expect**
- More intentional touching
- Longer focus (10-15 minutes)
- Anticipating object movement
- Starting to show preferences

**How to Help**
- Ask "Can you touch the red one?"
- Wait for them to respond
- Praise specific actions
- Follow their preferences

**Activities**
- Count touches together
- Choose themes together
- Point and touch games
- Color naming practice

#### 10-12 Months: Exploration Phase

**What to Expect**
- Very intentional interaction
- Extended play (15-20 minutes)
- Clear preferences
- Problem-solving attempts

**How to Help**
- Ask more complex questions
- Let them make choices
- Encourage decision-making
- Build on their interests

**Activities**
- Find specific colors/objects
- Count and identify
- Theme exploration
- Achievement tracking

## Troubleshooting

### Baby Not Interested

**Try:**
- Different time of day
- Shorter sessions
- Different theme
- More enthusiasm from you
- Break and try later

### Too Stimulated

**Solution:**
- Switch to Contrast World theme
- Reduce session length
- Take breaks
- End session calmly

### Not Touching Screen

**Remember:**
- Normal for youngest babies
- Let them watch first
- Guide their hand gently
- No pressure to perform
- They'll engage when ready

### Profile Issues

**Check:**
- Age is correct
- Name is saved
- Settings panel for overrides
- Refresh browser if needed
