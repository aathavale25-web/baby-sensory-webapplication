## ğŸ¯ Age-Specific Quick Start

### 4-6 Months

**Recommended First Session:**
1. Use **Contrast World** theme
2. Sit **12-18 inches** from screen
3. Play for **5-10 minutes**
4. Point to objects as they appear
5. Guide baby's hand gently

**What to Expect:**
- 1-2 large objects
- Black, white, red, yellow, blue colors
- Very slow movement
- Objects stay 3 seconds after touch

### 7-9 Months

**Recommended First Session:**
1. Try **Ocean** theme
2. Sit **18-24 inches** from screen
3. Play for **10-15 minutes**
4. Ask "Can you touch the fish?"
5. Celebrate each success

**What to Expect:**
- 3-5 objects
- More colors (green, orange, purple)
- Moderate speed
- Objects stay 2 seconds after touch

### 10-12 Months

**Recommended First Session:**
1. Explore **Rainbow** or **Space** theme
2. Sit **24-30 inches** from screen
3. Play for **15-20 minutes**
4. Let them choose what to touch
5. Count touches together

**What to Expect:**
- 5-8 objects
- Full color palette
- Fast, dynamic movement
- Objects disappear after celebration
